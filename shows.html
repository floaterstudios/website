Idealistic

Steven Andrews was a dreamer and an idealist at the ripe age of three and a half. He always believed that his life was destined for greatness. Once Steven physically grew up he realized the life he was hoping for...well, didn't happen. Steven Andrews tells the story of what he thought his life would be, how it didn't happen and how he coped with it by unexpectedly finding solace from his career as a medical professional.
