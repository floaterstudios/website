About

Floater Studios, an Albertan theatre company produces plays in a comedic monologue story-telling style.

Floater Studios arose from the ashes of Steven Andrews' mind in the year 2011. Steven runs Floater Studios as its Artistic Director, Head Imagineer, and lead actor. He holds a Drama and a Nursing degree from the University of Alberta. The company produces one-man shows and frequently collaborates with the amazing Eileen Sproule and the tender loving Luke Diehl. This thought provoking and humourous art hopes to laugh and move the audience through the use of themes such as existentialism, the ridiculous, the human condition, and love.
